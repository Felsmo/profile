About me

I have a background in Sales where I first came in contact with a group of programmers in Malaysia. I was fascinated to see how a dedicated team of programmers, testers and designers were able to create a tech solution that helped companies engage with their target audience. The idea to work in a team and create a custom made solutions from scratch appealed greatly to me.

Being in sales I always tried my best to discover the customers greatest challenges and needs, but not being able to personally build something has always felt to me as a missing part of my potential to add value. I hope that, during my time at Le Wagon, I will learn to get a deeper understanding of tech solutions and in the near future will be able to personally add to the right solution, instead of just selling products.
